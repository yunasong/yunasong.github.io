About Me

I'm Yuna Song, a fourth-year Environmental Studies and Visual Arts double major at the University of Chicago. I work as a Policy Intern for Sustainability and Resilience at the City Hall of Chicago, and on campus as a Communications Manager and barista at Ex Libris Café. Art is my favorite way of remembering, a medium through which I have freedom to remember pieces about my life through a unique and unapologetic perspective.
Sustainable fashion is my passion and personal long-term pursuit. This year I'm writing my B.A. thesis on the slow fashion movement in comparison to the values and practices of fast fashion at the levels of production, retail, and post-purchase. I envision the fashion industry as a field of great potential for change in terms of environmental conservation, human rights, and gender equality.
